How do I create an account?
In our site we do not offer you an option to create an account😔. Here's why:
1. Due to security concerns both in our end and your end.
2. Easy database control from our side, reduced back-end and server pressure.
3. This helps you be confident while using our services.
4. Though this might seem annoying to you as you cannot access your cart across devices.😔 Privacy to information is always a priority!🥰💌
