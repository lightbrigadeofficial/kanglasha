Wings Of Fire By A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Wings of Fire by A. P. J. Abdul Kalam is an inspiring autobiography that chronicles the life of India’s former President and renowned scientist. The book details his humble beginnings in a small town in Tamil Nadu, his education, and his rise to prominence in the Indian space and defense programs. Kalam shares his experiences working on pivotal projects, including the development of India's first satellite launch vehicle and missile systems. The autobiography also reflects his passion for education, innovation, and nation-building. With its emphasis on hard work, perseverance, and the pursuit of dreams, *Wings of Fire* serves as a motivating story of self-realization and contribution to society.
