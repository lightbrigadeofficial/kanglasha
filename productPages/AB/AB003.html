Gandhi My Experiments With Truth ( An Autobiography )

Gandhi: My Experiments with Truth is the autobiography of Mahatma Gandhi, in which he reflects on his personal journey of self-discovery, moral development, and political activism. Written over several years, the book is divided into sections that cover Gandhi's early life in India, his education in London, his time in South Africa fighting for civil rights, and his leadership in the Indian independence movement. Gandhi discusses his experiments with non-violence, truth, and self-discipline, along with his deep faith in simplicity and self-reliance. The autobiography offers insight into his spiritual and philosophical beliefs, his struggles with personal shortcomings, and his commitment to truth and justice as guiding principles in his life.
