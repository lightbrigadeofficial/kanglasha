Corrupt By Penelope Douglas (Devil’s Night Book 1)

Corrupt by Penelope Douglas is a dark, seductive romance that delves into secrets, revenge, and forbidden attraction. The story follows Erika "Rika" Fane, a young woman who becomes the target of Michael Crist, her ex-boyfriend's older brother, and his group of friends known as the Horsemen. Years after a fateful Devil's Night, Michael seeks revenge for the betrayal that tore their lives apart, but his obsession with Rika complicates his plans. With its intense chemistry, morally gray characters, and themes of power, desire, and redemption, *Corrupt* is a thrilling and provocative start to the *Devil’s Night* series.
