The Maddest Obsession By Danielle Lori

The Maddest Obsession by Danielle Lori is a dark and passionate romance set in a world of crime and obsession. The story revolves around Gianna, a free-spirited woman with a troubled past, and Christian Allister, a cold, calculating man deeply entrenched in the mafia underworld. Despite their differences and mutual resistance, an undeniable pull ties them together, leading to a dangerous and intoxicating relationship. With its intense chemistry, morally complex characters, and themes of power, desire, and redemption, the novel delivers a gripping tale of love that defies all boundaries.
