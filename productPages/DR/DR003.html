Kingdom Of The Wicked By Kerri Maniscalco

"Kingdom of the Wicked" by Kerri Maniscalco is a dark and enchanting fantasy about vengeance, magic, and forbidden love. The story follows Emilia, a young witch who seeks to uncover the truth behind her twin sister Vittoria’s brutal murder. Her quest leads her to summon Wrath, one of the wicked princes of Hell, with whom she forms a reluctant alliance. As Emilia delves deeper into the dangerous world of demons, secrets, and betrayal, she must confront her own inner darkness. With its lush world-building, gripping mystery, and sizzling tension, Kingdom of the Wicked is a spellbinding tale of love, loyalty, and revenge..
