Bound By Honor By Cora Reilly

Bound by Honor by Cora Reilly is a captivating mafia romance that explores duty, loyalty, and forbidden passion. The story follows Aria Scuderi, the eldest daughter of a powerful mafia family, who is forced into an arranged marriage with Luca Vitiello, the ruthless heir to the New York mafia. While their union is meant to strengthen alliances, Aria struggles with fear and mistrust, while Luca’s cold exterior hides unexpected depths. As they navigate their dangerous world, their relationship evolves from reluctant partnership to something deeper. With its intense emotions, high stakes, and forbidden allure, Bound by Honor is a gripping start to the Born in Blood Mafia Chronicles.
