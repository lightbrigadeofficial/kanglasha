Shatter Me By Tahereh Mafi

Shatter Me by Tahereh Mafi is a gripping dystopian novel about power, freedom, and self-discovery. It follows Juliette Ferrars, a 17-year-old girl whose touch is lethal, making her a prisoner of a corrupt government that seeks to use her as a weapon. Isolated and filled with self-doubt, Juliette’s life changes when she encounters Adam, a soldier who makes her feel seen, and Warner, a ruthless leader obsessed with her power. As she begins to understand her strength, Juliette must decide whether to be controlled or fight for her own freedom. With its poetic prose and emotional intensity, Shatter Me is a thrilling journey of resilience and rebellion.
