We Were Liars By E Lockhart

We Were Liars by E. Lockhart is a haunting and suspenseful tale of family, privilege, and hidden truths. The story revolves around Cadence Sinclair, a teenager from a wealthy family who spends her summers on their private island with her cousins and a close friend, collectively known as the Liars. After a mysterious accident leaves Cadence with memory loss and debilitating migraines, she returns to the island two years later, determined to uncover the truth about what happened. As fragmented memories resurface, shocking secrets are revealed, leading to a devastating climax. With its lyrical prose and emotional depth, We Were Liars is a powerful exploration of love, loss, and betrayal.
