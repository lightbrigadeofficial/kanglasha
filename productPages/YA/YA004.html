A Thousand Boy Kisses By Tillie Cole

A Thousand Boy Kisses by Tillie Cole is a heart-wrenching romance about love, loss, and the power of unforgettable memories. The story follows Rune Kristiansen and Poppy Litchfield, childhood best friends and soulmates, whose bond is tested when Rune moves away to Norway. Upon his return years later, he discovers Poppy has been hiding a heartbreaking secret. As they reconnect, they embark on fulfilling Poppy’s dream of collecting a thousand meaningful kisses, cherishing each moment despite their impending goodbye. With its emotional depth and tender storytelling, the novel captures the beauty of living and loving fully, even in the face of heartbreak.
