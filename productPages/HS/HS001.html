Mossad By Michael Bar-Zohar

Mossad by Michael Bar-Zohar is an insightful and thrilling account of the history and operations of Israel’s intelligence agency, the Mossad. The book delves into the agency's most significant and covert missions, highlighting its role in shaping the modern state of Israel. Through interviews, archival research, and first-hand accounts, Bar-Zohar reveals the inner workings of Mossad, from its strategic operations in the Middle East to its involvement in global espionage. The narrative provides a detailed look at the agents, their methods, and their motivations, offering a gripping exploration of intelligence, secrecy, and the moral dilemmas faced by those who serve in such a high-stakes environment.
