Dracula By Bram Stoker

Dracula by Bram Stoker is a classic Gothic horror novel that introduced the iconic vampire Count Dracula. The story unfolds through a series of letters, journal entries, and newspaper clippings, chronicling the efforts of a group of individuals to confront and defeat the dark and sinister Dracula. The novel begins with Jonathan Harker's journey to Dracula's castle in Transylvania, where he uncovers the Count's supernatural powers and malevolent plans. As Dracula travels to England, spreading terror and corruption, a band of brave men and women, led by Professor Van Helsing, work together to stop him. With its eerie atmosphere, themes of fear, desire, and the clash between modern science and ancient superstition, *Dracula* remains a timeless masterpiece of horror fiction.
