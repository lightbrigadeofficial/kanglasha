Every Summer After By Carley Fortune

Every Summer After by Carley Fortune is a poignant tale of love, loss, and second chances. The story follows Persephone “Percy” Fraser as she returns to the lakeside town where she spent her childhood summers and reconnects with Sam, her first love and best friend. Through alternating timelines, the novel explores their intense bond, the heartbreak that tore them apart, and the unresolved feelings that linger years later. With its vivid setting and deeply emotional narrative, the book captures the bittersweet beauty of youthful love and the enduring power of forgiveness.
