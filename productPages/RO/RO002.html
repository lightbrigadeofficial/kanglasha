The Love Hypothesis By Ali Hazelwood

The Love Hypothesis by Ali Hazelwood is a charming romantic comedy set in the world of academia. The story follows Olive Smith, a PhD candidate, who pretends to date Dr. Adam Carlsen, a young and intimidating professor, to convince her friends she’s moved on from a past relationship. What starts as a fake relationship evolves into something more as Olive and Adam navigate their growing feelings, academic pressures, and personal insecurities. With its witty banter, heartfelt moments, and themes of ambition, love, and self-worth, the novel delivers a delightful and empowering take on modern romance.
