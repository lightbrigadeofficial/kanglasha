Mission NDA : Serve At Young Age (National Defence Academy Entrance Examination) | National Defence Academy Khadakwasla

Mission NDA: Serve at a Young Age is a guidebook aimed at aspirants preparing for the National Defence Academy (NDA) entrance examination. The book provides detailed insights into the NDA entrance exam process, covering subjects like mathematics, general knowledge, and English, which are essential for success. It includes tips on how to enhance physical fitness, improve mental toughness, and develop discipline—key attributes for those pursuing a career in the Indian Armed Forces. Additionally, the book offers strategies for cracking the written exam and the Services Selection Board (SSB) interview. Through this comprehensive resource, it helps guide young aspirants towards achieving their goal of joining the prestigious NDA in Khadakwasla, with a focus on service and leadership at a young age.
