Steve Jobs By Walter Isaacson

Steve Jobs by Walter Isaacson is a comprehensive biography of the iconic Apple co-founder, Steve Jobs. Based on extensive interviews with Jobs, his family, friends, colleagues, and competitors, the book provides an in-depth look at his visionary approach to technology, design, and business. It explores his complex personality, ranging from his genius and creativity to his often abrasive management style. The biography details Jobs' journey in building Apple, his time at Pixar, and his eventual return to lead Apple to unprecedented success with revolutionary products like the iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Through this detailed narrative, *Steve Jobs* presents the man behind the myth, capturing both his brilliance and flaws, and how his vision changed the world.
