Act Like A Lady Think Like A Man By Steve Harvey

Act Like a Lady, Think Like a Man by Steve Harvey is a witty and candid relationship guide that offers insights into how men think and behave in romantic relationships. Drawing from his own experiences and years of giving relationship advice, Harvey shares practical advice to help women understand the mindset of men when it comes to love, commitment, and family. With humor and straight talk, he addresses topics like how men view relationships, the importance of standards, and what it takes to build a lasting connection. This book serves as a no-nonsense manual for women looking to navigate the complexities of modern dating and relationships.
