Atomic Habits by James Clear

Atomic Habits by James Clear is a self-help book that explores how small, incremental changes can lead to significant personal and professional transformations. Clear emphasizes the importance of building good habits and breaking bad ones through simple, actionable strategies. The book offers practical advice on how to make lasting changes by focusing on systems rather than goals, making it a valuable guide for anyone seeking to improve their daily routines and overall effectiveness.
