Can't Hurt Me By David Goggins

In "Can't Hurt Me," David Goggins, a former Navy SEAL and ultramarathon runner, shares his life story of overcoming extreme hardship, abuse, and self-doubt to become one of the world's toughest endurance athletes. Through his concept of the "40% Rule," Goggins teaches readers how to push past mental and physical barriers to unlock their true potential. This raw and powerful memoir is a guide to mental resilience and relentless self-improvement, inspiring readers to embrace discomfort, face their fears, and conquer the impossible.
