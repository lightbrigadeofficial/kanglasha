[1] Harry Potter And the Philosopher’s Stone By J. K. Rowling

Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone by J.K. Rowling is the first book in the iconic Harry Potter series. It follows the journey of an orphaned boy, Harry Potter, who discovers on his 11th birthday that he is a wizard. Invited to study at the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Harry uncovers secrets about his parents, his unique destiny, and the magical world. Alongside his friends Ron and Hermione, he faces thrilling adventures, including the mystery of the Philosopher’s Stone and the dark forces surrounding it. This enchanting tale is a timeless introduction to the magic of friendship, bravery, and discovery.
