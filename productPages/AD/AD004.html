[3] Harry Potter And The Prisoner Of Azkaban By J. K. Rowling

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban by J.K. Rowling is the third spellbinding book in the Harry Potter series. This time, Harry faces a new challenge when Sirius Black, a notorious prisoner from Azkaban, escapes and is rumored to be coming after him. As the dark secrets of Sirius's past are unveiled, Harry discovers shocking truths about his family, his connection to the escaped convict, and the betrayal that led to his parents' deaths. With the magical Marauder's Map, the introduction of the mysterious Dementors, and thrilling encounters with time travel, this book weaves a captivating tale of friendship, trust, and courage.
