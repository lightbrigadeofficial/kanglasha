[6] Harry Potter And The Half Blood Prince By J. K. Rowling

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince is the sixth book in J.K. Rowling's iconic series. The story follows Harry Potter as he returns to Hogwarts for his sixth year, where he uncovers more about Lord Voldemort's past and his dark secrets. With the Wizarding World in turmoil and Voldemort's forces gaining strength, Harry begins private lessons with Professor Dumbledore to learn about Voldemort's Horcruxes—objects containing parts of his soul. The novel explores themes of love, loss, and loyalty, leading to shocking twists, particularly the tragic death of a beloved character. The book sets the stage for the final battle between good and evil in the series.
