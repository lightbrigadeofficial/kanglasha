Mission CDS : Combined Defence Services Entrance Examination for IMA, INA & AFA

Mission CDS: Combined Defence Services Entrance Examination for IMA, INA & AFA is a comprehensive guidebook designed for aspirants preparing for the Combined Defence Services (CDS) exam. The book focuses on the entrance requirements for the Indian Military Academy (IMA), Indian Naval Academy (INA), and Air Force Academy (AFA). It provides detailed coverage of the subjects tested in the CDS exam, including English, General Knowledge, and Elementary Mathematics. The book also offers practical tips for improving time management, exam strategy, and physical fitness, which are essential for success. In addition to exam preparation, it includes guidance for the Services Selection Board (SSB) interview, helping candidates prepare mentally and physically for a career in the Indian Armed Forces. Through structured content and expert insights, the book aims to help aspirants achieve success in the CDS exam and begin their journey toward serving in the Indian Army, Navy, or Air Force.
